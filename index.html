Who We Are

Built on Belief.
Driven by Purpose.

Founded on September 23, 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation headquartered in Chhattisgarh, working tirelessly across all corners of India to create lasting social impact.

Under the leadership of our Founder & CEO, Mr. Govind Shukla, we operate with full institutional credibility — registered under 80G & 12A, CSR-1 certified, enlisted with NITI Aayog, and proudly holding the IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification.

From food kitchens to classrooms, from forest floors to skill centres — every programme we run is a step toward an India that leaves no one behind.

Our Initiatives

Six Causes.
One Mission.

Every project we run is designed to address a real, urgent need — and together, they form the full picture of the India we are working towards.

🍱

Project Seva

Nourishment & Dignity

Providing nutritious food and essential clothing to underprivileged individuals across communities — because dignity begins with the basics.

📚

Project Bachpanshala

Education for Every Child

Bringing quality education to children who have been denied access — building futures one classroom, one child, one lesson at a time.

🐾

Project Jeev

Animal Rescue & Care

Rescuing, protecting, and feeding stray animals — because compassion has no species boundary and every life deserves kindness.

🌸

Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Equipping women with skills, knowledge, and confidence to achieve financial independence and claim their rightful place in society.

🌿

Project Prakriti

Environmental Conservation

Planting trees, raising environmental awareness, and driving sustainability initiatives to preserve the natural world for generations to come.

🚀

Project Vikas

Skill & Employability

Empowering youth through structured skill-development programmes that build employability and unlock economic opportunity across India.
